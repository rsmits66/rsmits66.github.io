Sphere trigonometry — isosceles cone builder

Given angles θ₁, …, θₙ with defect Ω = 2π − Σθᵢ > 0, find r such that Σ arcsin(sin(θᵢ/2) / r) = π. Then d = √(1 − r²) and the base vertices sit at (r cos φᵢ, r sin φᵢ, −d) on the unit sphere.
