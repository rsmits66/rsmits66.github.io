1When α = β = 1 the expression collapses to the standard logistic density with location μ and scale σ. The two shape parameters α and β separately govern the right- and left-tail decay, respectively, allowing skewness without giving up the closed form.

2The mode lies at x = μ + σ·log(β/α). The right tail decays as exp(−αz) and the left tail as exp(βz), where z = (x−μ)/σ. Larger α flattens the right side; larger β flattens the left.

3The density is evaluated in log-space using a Lanczos approximation for log Γ and a numerically stable softplus for log(1+e−z), so extreme parameter combinations remain well-behaved.
