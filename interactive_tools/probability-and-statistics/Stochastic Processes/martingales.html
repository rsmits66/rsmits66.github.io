how to read the matrix
below diagonalthe forward direction — conditioning on the past, predicting the future. For each of these three processes the empirical curve should ride the dashed 45° line. That is the martingale.
above diagonalthe backward direction — conditioning on the future. Shrinkage toward zero. Slope is s/t for B and the compensated Poisson, and (s/t)² for the quadratic.
diagonalmarginal histogram, tinted in the process's accent.
the gold pathone single realization, highlighted across every cell — appears as a gold dot in each scatter, a gold notch on every histogram, and a continuous trajectory in Fig. II. The vertical guides on Fig. II mark the chosen times; their values are exactly the gold dots in Fig. I.
